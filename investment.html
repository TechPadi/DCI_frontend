Investment Scheme

DCI investment scheme is open to all members of the public, it does not permit the participation of the investor in the day to day running of the business or entity; the investor only receives 3.5% - 8% interest monthly depending on the invested sum for the duration of 12 months. Upon the completion of the said 12 months, the capital shall be returned to the investor with an option of renewal.
