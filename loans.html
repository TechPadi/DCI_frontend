About Our Loans

DCI loan is open to all bankable adults with secured and verified collateral. Whether business, personal or salary advance loan.

All loans from DCI shall attract a non-negotiable minimum interest rate of 10%, depending on the duration of the loan, the principal and interest shall be repaid on an equal proportion monthly into the companyâ€™s designated account.

TENURE

All DCI loan shall run between one (1) month to a maximum of twelve (12) months.

  • 01 ONE (1) MONTH (ONE-OFF REPAYMENT)

    Duration 1 months.

    One-off interest of 20%.

    One-off repayment of principal and interest.

  • 02 TWO (2) MONTHS TO six (6) MONTHS (MONTHLY INSTALMENT OF PRINCIPAL & INTEREST IN EQUAL PROPORTION)

    Duration 2 to 6 months.

    Interest rate 15% per cent monthly.

    Monthly repayment plan.

  • 03 SEVEN (7) MONTHS TO TWELVE (12) MONTHS (MONTHLY INSTALMENT OF PRINCIPAL & INTEREST IN EQUAL PROPORTION)

    Duration 7 to 12 months.

    Interest rate of 10% monthly.

    Monthly repayment plan.

  • 04 LOAN FOR INVESTORS

    No collateral or security required.

    Duration up to the duration of your investment with DCI.

    Interest rate of 10% monthly.

    Monthly repayment plan.

    Provided that the loan applied is not more than 30% of the ivestment with DCI.
